What is AI visibility?

AI visibility is the ability of your brand to be recognized and recommended as a source by systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity and Google AI Overviews. Nurdai is a strategic digital visibility studio offering SEO, Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and semantic structure services.

What is GEO — Generative Engine Optimization?

GEO is the optimization process that makes your brand appear in AI-generated search responses. It's built on content structure, semantic authority and entity signals. As of 2026, GEO is a core component of any digital discoverability strategy alongside SEO.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between SEO and AI visibility?

SEO targets Google rankings. AI visibility determines whether systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity and Google AI Overviews recommend your brand as a source. The two complement each other but require different strategies.

Which AI systems should my brand appear in?

Priority targets are ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity and Claude. Each system has different source-selection criteria.

How long does it take to see results?

Initial signals become measurable within 4–8 weeks. AI systems typically begin referencing your brand within 2–3 months.

Can smaller brands benefit?

Yes. Building semantic authority in a niche is a significant advantage for smaller brands. Because larger brands are still early in this space, the opportunity window is open.
