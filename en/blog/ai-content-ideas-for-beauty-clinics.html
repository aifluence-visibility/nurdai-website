Clinics that answer honestly are treated as more trustworthy sources.

📍 Local Search Intent

AI questions aren't limited to the service, treatment, or product itself. People also ask AI which business to choose. Content addressing that is just as valuable, for example:

  • How do I find a trustworthy beauty clinic in Bodrum?
  • Which treatments are recommended before summer?
  • Is there a clinic offering doctor-supervised Botox?
  • Is the first consultation free?
  • Which treatments give fast results for visitors on vacation?

💡 NurdAI Note

Trust is the deciding factor in aesthetics and beauty. AI favors content that's honest about risk, process, and realistic outcomes over content that overpromises.
