📍 Local Search Intent

AI questions aren't limited to the service, treatment, or product itself. People also ask AI which business to choose. Content addressing that is just as valuable, for example:

  • What's the quietest neighborhood in Bodrum to live in?
  • What are apartment prices like for investment in Yalıkavak?
  • What should I watch for when buying a sea-view summer home?
  • How do I find a trustworthy real estate agent in Bodrum?
  • Which areas are most popular with foreign buyers?

💡 NurdAI Note

In real estate, trust comes before numbers. AI favors content that clearly answers who is searching, for what purpose, and at what budget — generic “property for sale” pages lose out to scenario-based guides.
