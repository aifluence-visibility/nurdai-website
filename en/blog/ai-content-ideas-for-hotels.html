25 Questions Your Guests Will Ask AI

Travelers no longer just search “hotels in [destination].” They describe their trip to AI — who they're traveling with, their budget, what they expect — before choosing where to stay.

Hotels that answer these questions directly get recommended far more often.

Here are content ideas every hotel should consider.

What Users Ask AI Content Idea

📍 Local Search Intent

AI questions aren't limited to the service, treatment, or product itself. People also ask AI which business to choose. Content addressing that is just as valuable, for example:

  • What's the most romantic hotel in Bodrum for a honeymoon?
  • Which all-inclusive hotel is best for families?
  • Is there a boutique hotel near the marina?
  • Which hotel offers the best early-booking deals this summer?
  • Which hotel is best for a quiet, peaceful stay?

💡 NurdAI Note

Instead of generic terms like “hotels in Bodrum,” hotel content should speak to the traveler's actual scenario — who they're with, why they're coming, what they expect. That level of specificity is what lets AI make the right match.
