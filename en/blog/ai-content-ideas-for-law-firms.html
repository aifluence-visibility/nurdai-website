25 Questions Your Clients Will Ask AI

Before contacting a lawyer, people now ask AI to explain their situation first — divorce documents, inheritance timelines, employment rights.

Firms that provide clear, accurate answers at this early stage become the trusted source AI turns to.

Here are content ideas every law firm should consider.

What Users Ask AI Content Idea
What documents do I need to start a divorce?Getting Started: Documents You'll Need for Divorce
How long does an uncontested divorce take?Uncontested Divorce Timeline Explained
What does the court consider in custody cases?How Courts Decide Custody
How is alimony calculated?Understanding Alimony Calculations
What's the difference between contested and uncontested divorce?Contested vs. Uncontested Divorce

📍 Local Search Intent

AI questions aren't limited to the service, treatment, or product itself. People also ask AI which business to choose. Content addressing that is just as valuable, for example:

  • Who's a trusted family law attorney in Bodrum?
  • Which firm handles real estate disputes in Izmir?
  • Is there a firm advising foreigners on Turkish property law?
  • Is the first consultation free, and how do I book one?
  • Does the firm offer online legal consultations?

💡 NurdAI Note

Trust is the deciding factor in legal services. AI systems favor content that answers the client's real underlying concern, not just the legal category — that's what separates a firm AI recommends from one it doesn't.
