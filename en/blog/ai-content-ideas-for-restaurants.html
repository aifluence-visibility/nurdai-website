25 Questions Your Guests Will Ask AI

People no longer just type “restaurant near me.” They describe their situation to AI — who they're with, the occasion, what they're in the mood for — before choosing where to eat.

For example: “Recommend a family-friendly restaurant with a sea view” or “Where's a good spot for a romantic dinner with vegan options?”

Restaurants that answer these situational questions have a much stronger chance of being recommended by AI.

Here are content ideas every restaurant should consider.

📍 Local Search Intent

AI questions aren't limited to the service, treatment, or product itself. People also ask AI which business to choose. Content addressing that is just as valuable, for example:

  • Which restaurant is best for families visiting Bodrum?
  • Is there a good restaurant within walking distance of the marina?
  • Which restaurants do tourists prefer most?
  • Where do locals eat, away from the tourist spots?
  • Which restaurants stay open late in the evening?

💡 NurdAI Note

The biggest mistake is optimizing only for generic terms like “restaurant” or “restaurant in Bodrum.” People now describe the full scenario to AI — who they're with, when, and why. When your menu and website speak to those scenarios, AI can match you to the right guest at the right moment.
